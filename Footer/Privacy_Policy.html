Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Pokém-Info website! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any data on our site. Please note that this is a frontend-only project, meaning we do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users.

2. Information We Collect

As a frontend-only website, we do not collect or store any personal information. We do not have a backend or database to process user data. Any interaction you have with the site, including using the Pokémon API to fetch data and AI-generated Pokémon images, does not involve storing personal data.

3. AI-Generated Pokémon Images

Our website uses artificial intelligence to generate Pokémon images based on user interactions. These images are for visual representation purposes only and are not linked to any personal information. No data is stored regarding your image generation or interactions on the site.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies to collect any personal information or track users. Cookies may be used for basic functionality, such as ensuring a smooth user experience while navigating through the site, but no personal data is stored.

5. Third-Party Services

Our site uses the Pokémon API to retrieve Pokémon data (such as abilities, types, evolutions, and other information). However, we do not store or share any data retrieved from the API. The Pokémon API is only used to display data on the website in real-time and does not collect any personal information.

6. Data Security

As we do not store any personal data, there is no risk to your privacy in terms of data security. Our website is a frontend-only application, meaning there is no backend infrastructure to manage or secure user information.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store any personal information, there are no rights related to accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal data. You can simply choose not to interact with the website or use its features if you prefer.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are any changes, we will update the "Effective Date" above. Please review this page periodically for any updates. As we do not store any data, changes to the policy will have minimal impact on your experience with the site.
